Why Should you invest?

You know those products and brands we love spending our money on? Did you know that when you purchase something from them you're basically investing in their company SO why not invest in their shares.

Dividend Rewards

Some companies will give a share of their profits, called dividends, to their shareholders (that's you!) which can be used to buy more shares or be taken as a cash pay-out. Money for nothing!

Shared Value Increase

When the company you have invested in grows and makes more profit, its value goes up. When you sell your share in the business, it's worth more than what you paid for it. You make moolah!
